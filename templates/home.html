What is PlaceHub?

PlaceHub is a comprehensive placement management system designed for educational institutions. We bridge the gap between talented students and forward-thinking companies, making campus recruitment transparent, efficient, and accessible.

Our platform uses role-based access control to ensure students focus on opportunities, companies manage talent effectively, and administrators maintain full oversight — all in one place.
